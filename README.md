# SriPatro
This is a concise exact day to day panchang details accurate upto minutes. The word `Panchang` means to have 5 Components of The Day according to Vedic Astrology.

These 5 Components are 

| Component | Signifiance |
| --------- | ----------- |
| 1. Tithi | Lunar Day |
| 2. Vaara | Day of The Week |
| 3. Nakshatra | Constilation |
| 4. Yoga | distance between the sun and moon |
| 5. Karana | Half Lunar Day |
| 6. Paksha | Waning or Waxing Moon|
| 7. Raasi | Moon Sign |

## Panchanng from Bikram Sambat and Anno Domini

> Bikram Sambat (BS) Date
[ 🔗 SriPatro/nepalitoenglish](https://sripatro.vercel.app/nepalitoenglish)

Nepali to English Date Converter, converts Nepali (Bikram Sambat, BS) date to English (Gregorian date, AD). It can convert date range of 2000 B.S to 2089 B.S. This tool is useful for converting dates like date of birth and historical nepali dates, alsoGetting the full day panchang for theSameDay.

> Anno Domini (AD) date
[ 🔗 SriPatro/birthpanchang](https://sripatro.vercel.app/birthpanchang)

Easely get Panchang from Bikram Sambat (BS) Date or Anno Dommini (AD) Date, Just Enter the Date in Date Input ` Date, Month and Year ` and `Time` in 24 Hrs Format. you will get Panchang for Full Day.
